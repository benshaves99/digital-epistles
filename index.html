The bible is God's love story with his creation in how he created us, how we have fallen from his grace and how he redeemed us through his son. Paul's letters to the churches, or the epistles, are in some ways love letters to these different communities around Israel. Although he can be harsh and challenge what the churches are doing wrong it is all out of love and wanting them to represent Christ as best as they can.
